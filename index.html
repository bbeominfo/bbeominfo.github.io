Who I am

I'm a graduate student pursuing dual master's degrees in Computational Science & Engineering and Bioinformatics at Georgia Tech. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning and biomedical science — building AI systems that can make sense of complex biological data.

I'm particularly drawn to large language models and their applications in the biomedical domain — from clinical text understanding to drug discovery and genomics. I care about building systems that are not only accurate, but interpretable and practically deployable.
